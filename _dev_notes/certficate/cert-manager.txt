kubernetes clusters uses cert-manager service for PKI certification and rotation of internal certificate used by Linkerd, and ingress, and some outbound traffic to external databases

* Creating Root certificate
from the monorepo root
run `step certificate create root.cluster.local ca.crt ca.key --profile root-ca`
